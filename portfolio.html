PORTFOLIO

We’ve transformed a living-ready home turned over by a developer into a standout, extraordinary home. We provided advice on the materials selection for the new home design to guide the client and ensure that they get the most out of their purchase and achieve their vision. We then took on the furnishing to achieve a calming neutral look. We customized every furniture, made bespoke pieces through our joinery services, and selected accessories such as curtains and sheets, and finished the entire look with natural colors and elements.
